Platform Features

Everything a Home Entrepreneur Needs

From a seller's first conversation with Jami to a live product listing — the entire journey in one integrated platform.

WhatsApp-Native Onboarding
No app downloads, no forms, no jargon. Sellers interact with Jami entirely through a familiar chat interface — in English or Malayalam.
Real Margin Validation
Jami asks the right questions — including costs sellers routinely forget (packaging, wastage, own time) — and validates genuine profitability before any product goes live.
Instant Product Listings
Once validated, a mobile-ready product landing page is auto-generated with the seller's story, photos, and a direct WhatsApp order button.
Government-Facing Dashboard
Kudumbashree and LSGD teams get a clean ops view — every seller's journey, product status, and margin health in one credible, professional interface.
Malayalam First
Jami converses natively in Malayalam — not a translation layer on top of English, but responses generated directly in the language sellers live and think in.
Dignified, Not Charity
Every interaction is designed to feel aspirational and professional — for both the seller and the government stakeholder viewing the same system.
The Journey

Homemaker to Entrepreneur in Four Steps

A seller's complete journey — from first greeting to validated product on the market.

Meet Jami
A seller opens the chat. Jami greets her in Malayalam or English, learns her name and district, and begins the partnership.
Product Intake
Jami walks through product description, photos, and all costs — probing specifically for what most sellers undercount.
Margin Validation
Jami calculates gross margin. If ≥ 100%, the product is validated and celebrated. If not, she coaches on pricing — no rejection, just guidance.
Go Live
A branded product listing is auto-generated. The seller appears in the government dashboard instantly, ready for orders via WhatsApp.
